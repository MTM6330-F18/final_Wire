Professor

Name : Ken McGinn
Course : Applied Projects

Place of Study: Ran a photography company called “Extreme Canada”, Algonquin College IMD course for 3 years

Teaching Duration: Nearly 8 Years teaching IMD, started by teaching Photography 1 and Video 1, and moved on to Applied Projects after 4 years

He has worked in the industry since 1988, starting with self promotion of his professional bike racing

Currently in Industry?: Yes


When asked what Ken liked about teaching the course he said this; “Connecting with students on Linkedin is one way I can go now and see the students i've been involved with and I an see the jobs they are doing now and I can almost tell you ‘that students going to go on and make a lot of money in a couple of years. I see a lot of success stories on Linkedin.’ Another favourite part is that I am being paid to learn, and I always like researching things. In IMD you aren’t really specializing in anything, but the really good students come out of here knowing the whole picture, they know how to go into anything involving media and they will know how the team works. We can see the whole picture, we know how to work with the coders, we know how to work with the designers, and you know how to optimize your talent.”
